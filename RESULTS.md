## Validation

The numbers above are backed by **180 passing tests** (`pytest tests/`,
~4 min). Coverage relevant to result correctness:

* **Cross-validation** вЂ” NCT vs П‡ВІ converse, log-domain vs linear path
  (agreement ~10вЃ»вЃ¶ bits/use), scalar vs vectorised Lemma 1, simple vs
  PPV-faithful ОєОІ, exact Monte-Carlo random coding vs the RCUвЃє integral.
* **Published reference points** вЂ” Gallager `n=3000, Оµ=10вЃ»вЃ¶ в†’ log M = 1225`;
  ОєОІ_PPV ОІ-formula matches Polyanskiy's `betaq_up_v2.m`.
* **Round-trip identities** вЂ” `achievable_error(achievable_rate(Оµ)) в‰€ Оµ`;
  the `log F` interpolator is exact at its grid nodes.
* **Monotonicity** of every bound in `n`, `Оµ`, `SNR`, `R`.
* **Tail extension** вЂ” the log-domain path stays finite where the linear path
  underflows to zero; `log_nct_cdf` cross-checked against `scipy.stats.t` at
  `nc = 0`; solid-angle vs NCT below 10вЃ»В№вЃ° relative error at small n.
